ABOUT

Bent Spoon Games is a (not yet incorporated) company founded by Alexei Andreev in 2011. The purpose of this company is to create video games based on ideas of rationality and transhumanism. Furthermore, I will try to apply the principles of rationality - rather than discourse - to the game design discipline. I will try to create games and promote game design values that don't simply lead to good games, but also promote awareness of complicated issues that exist in game design: sexism, racism, addiction, and valuing player's time.

A little bit about me. I've started programming in QBasic when I was 13 years old. From simple text-based games, to short animations, to interactive games, to 3D engines: I've navigated, learned, and experimented, mostly on my own. I spent most of my free time during high school and college working on my own games and game related projects. I've graduated from University of Illinois with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science in 2009. After that I've worked for over a year at (now defunct) Budcat Creations.
