ABOUT

Bent Spoon Games is a (not yet incorporated) company founded by Alexei Andreev in 2011. The purpose of this company is to create video games based on ideas of rationalityand transhumanism . Furthermore, I will try to apply the principles of rationality - rather than discourse - to the game design discipline. I will try to create games and promote game design values that don't simply lead to good games, but also promote awareness of complicated issues that exist in game design: sexism, racism, addiction, and valuing player's time.
